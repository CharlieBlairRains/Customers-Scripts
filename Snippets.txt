>
>We need to get explicit consent from you to record this sensitive data on our National database in order for us to help you with your enquiry.
>
>1. we will only access it if we have good reason to do so;
>2. will only share what is relevant and necessary and then only with your consent;
>3. won't sell it to commercial organisations

>Are you happy for us to keep a record about this as part of your case record?
